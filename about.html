ABOUT





I have been interested in makeup since I was very young, doing makeup for the skating group I was part of, and for local theatre groups and school productions. I practised makeup constantly, developing my style and sense of self expression, and went on to learn Fashion Makeup Techniques at West Thames College when I was 17.

I moved to Brighton the year afterwards, to study for my Physics Masters degree. (Which as people often tell me, is very different from makeup!) During this time I continued makeup on the side, doing makeup for events and festivals, film, catwalk, and photoshoots. I graduated with my Masters in Physics in the summer of 2018.

I have now moved to London, pursuing makeup full-time, aiming to develop my skills even further. My focus is on the expressive, colourful or disturbing, and mainly fashion (catwalk or editorial), short films and music videos. I am based in London but willing to travel throughout the south of England.
